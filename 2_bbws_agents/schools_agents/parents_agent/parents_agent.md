**Version**: 1.0.0

**Purpose**: Empower parents to actively participate in their child's education through progress monitoring, school communication, and resource access

## Agent Overview

The Parents Agent is a dedicated AI assistant designed to support parents in managing their child's educational journey, providing tools for progress monitoring, teacher communication, school event participation, and academic support resources.

### Core Capabilities

1. **Student Progress Monitoring**
2. **Teacher & School Communication**
3. **Academic Support & Resources**
4. **College & Career Planning**
5. **Special Education Support**
6. **School Event & Volunteer Management**
7. **Multi-Child Management**

---

## 1. Student Progress Monitoring

### Capabilities
- Real-time grade access
- Attendance tracking and alerts
- Assignment due dates and reminders
- Test/quiz schedule awareness
- Progress report interpretation
- Goal setting and tracking
- Trend analysis and early warning

### Workflows

#### Daily Progress Check
```
1. Log into parent portal
2. Review today's updates
   ├── New grades posted
   ├── Assignment submissions
   ├── Teacher comments
   └── Upcoming deadlines
3. Check attendance status
4. Review behavior notes
5. Take action if needed
```

#### Weekly Progress Review
```
1. Review all current grades by subject
2. Check for missing assignments
3. Review recent test/quiz scores
4. Monitor attendance pattern
5. Read teacher communications
6. Identify areas needing support
7. Plan home support activities
8. Schedule teacher communication if needed
```

### Parent Dashboard Features
- **Grade Overview**: Current grades across all subjects
- **Assignments**: Upcoming due dates and missing work
- **Attendance**: Daily attendance and tardy tracking
- **Behavior**: Positive notes and concerns
- **Teacher Messages**: Unread communications
- **Calendar**: Tests, projects, school events

## 2. Teacher & School Communication

### Capabilities
- Direct messaging with teachers
- Conference scheduling
- Communication history tracking
- Translation services (multilingual support)
- Automated notifications
- Response tracking

### Workflows

#### Contacting a Teacher
```
1. Identify question or concern
2. Check FAQ/resources first
3. Navigate to teacher contact
4. Compose message
   ├── Subject line (clear and specific)
   ├── Student name and period/class
   ├── Specific question or concern
   └── Preferred response method
5. Send message
6. Expect response within 24-48 hours
7. Follow up if no response within timeframe
```

#### Scheduling Parent-Teacher Conference
```
1. Determine need for conference
   ├── Academic concerns
   ├── Behavior issues
   ├── IEP/504 review
   └── General check-in
2. Request conference via portal
3. Select preferred time slots
4. Specify meeting format (in-person/virtual)
5. Receive confirmation
6. Prepare questions and topics
7. Attend conference
8. Document action items
9. Follow up on commitments
```

### Communication Templates
- **Academic Question**: "I noticed [student] is struggling with..."
- **Missing Assignment**: "Can you help me understand what's needed for..."
- **Behavior Concern**: "I wanted to follow up on the behavior note from..."
- **Thank You**: "Thank you for your extra help with..."
- **Conference Request**: "I'd like to schedule a conference to discuss..."

## 5. Special Education Support

### Capabilities
- IEP/504 plan access and understanding
- Accommodations monitoring
- Progress monitoring on IEP goals
- Parent rights education
- Advocacy resources
- Service provider communication
- Transition planning support

### Workflows

#### Understanding Your Child's IEP
```
1. Access IEP document in portal
2. Review key sections
   ├── Present levels of performance
   ├── Annual goals and objectives
   ├── Special education services
   ├── Accommodations and modifications
   └── Progress reporting schedule
3. Ask questions if unclear
4. Understand your parental rights
5. Monitor implementation
```

#### Preparing for IEP Meeting
```
1. Review current IEP (30 days before)
2. Gather evidence
   ├── Work samples
   ├── Progress reports
   ├── Outside evaluations
   └── Communication logs
3. List concerns and priorities
4. Prepare questions for team
5. Identify goals for next year
6. Bring support person if desired
7. Attend meeting prepared
8. Participate actively
9. Review final IEP before signing
10. Request copy for records
```

### Parent Rights
- Right to participate in IEP meetings
- Right to request evaluations
- Right to review records
- Right to independent evaluation
- Right to prior written notice
- Right to dispute resolution

## Best Practices for Parents

### Communication
1. **Respond Promptly**: Reply to teacher messages within 24-48 hours
2. **Be Respectful**: Maintain professional, positive tone
3. **Be Specific**: Provide details and examples
4. **Follow Chain**: Contact teacher first, then counselor, then administrator
5. **Document**: Keep records of important communications

### Monitoring
1. **Check Daily**: Review portal daily or at least 3x per week
2. **Be Proactive**: Address concerns early
3. **Celebrate Success**: Acknowledge improvements and achievements
4. **Set Expectations**: Establish family expectations for grades
5. **Partner**: Work collaboratively with school

### Support at Home
1. **Routine**: Establish consistent homework time and space
2. **Expectations**: Set clear academic and behavioral expectations
3. **Resources**: Provide necessary supplies and support
4. **Balance**: Ensure time for sleep, play, and family
5. **Model**: Demonstrate value of education

### Engagement
1. **Attend**: Participate in school events when possible
2. **Volunteer**: Contribute time or resources
3. **Advocate**: Speak up for your child's needs appropriately
4. **Connect**: Build relationships with teachers and staff
5. **Participate**: Join PTA/PTO or school committees

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Can't Access Portal**
1. Verify username/email
2. Reset password
3. Check email for activation link
4. Contact school registrar

**Not Seeing Child's Information**
1. Verify child is linked to account
2. Check permissions with school
3. Ensure child is actively enrolled
4. Contact IT support

**Not Receiving Notifications**
1. Check notification settings
2. Verify email address is correct
3. Check spam/junk folder
4. Update notification preferences
