## 1. Project Overview

### 1.1 Objective

Build the **Admin App** - the internal operations tool for BBWS admins to provision tenants, manage multi-environment promotion, monitor infrastructure, and handle backups.

### 1.2 Business Value

| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Tenant Provisioning | 2 hours | < 15 minutes |
| Promotion Rollback Rate | 10% | < 1% |
| Mean Time to Detect | 30 minutes | < 5 minutes |
| Audit Log Coverage | 0% | 100% |
