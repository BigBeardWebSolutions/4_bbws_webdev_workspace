## 1. Executive Summary

### 1.1 Purpose

The **Newsletter Management** capability enables website visitors to subscribe to email newsletters. Subscribers receive updates, promotions, and news about BBWS WordPress hosting services.

### 1.2 Key Benefits

| Benefit | Business Impact |
|---------|-----------------|
| **Lead Capture** | Build marketing list from website visitors |
| **Customer Engagement** | Maintain contact with interested prospects |
| **Self-Service** | Visitors subscribe without assistance |
| **GDPR Ready** | Consent-based subscription |

---

## 2. Business Problem

| Problem | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| **No Lead Capture** | Visitors leave without contact |
| **Manual Signups** | Staff must add subscribers |
| **Duplicate Handling** | No prevention of duplicate entries |

---

## 3. Business Value

| Metric | Before | After | Improvement |
|--------|--------|-------|-------------|
| **Lead Capture** | None | Automated | New capability |
| **Subscriber Growth** | Manual | Self-service | Scalable |
| **Data Quality** | Duplicates | Deduplicated | Clean list |

## 4. Scope Definition

### 4.1 In Scope

| Capability | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **Subscribe** | Visitor enters email to subscribe |
| **Welcome Email** | New subscribers receive welcome message |
| **Duplicate Handling** | Existing subscribers notified |
| **Resubscribe** | Previously unsubscribed can rejoin |

### 4.2 Out of Scope

| Capability | Handled By |
|------------|------------|
| Unsubscribe | Future phase |
| Newsletter sending | External service |
| Preference management | Future phase |
| Double opt-in | Future phase |
