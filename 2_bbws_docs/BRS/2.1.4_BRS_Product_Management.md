## 1. Executive Summary

### 1.1 Purpose

The **Product Management** capability enables the display and management of WordPress hosting packages available for purchase through the Customer Portal. Customers browse products to understand pricing, features, and select the package that best meets their needs.

### 1.2 Key Benefits

| Benefit | Business Impact |
|---------|-----------------|
| **Self-Service Browsing** | Customers explore products without sales assistance |
| **Clear Pricing** | Transparent pricing builds customer trust |
| **Feature Comparison** | Customers make informed decisions |
| **Easy Updates** | Admins manage products without technical support |

---

## 2. Business Problem

| Problem | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| **No Product Catalog** | Customers cannot see available hosting options |
| **Manual Price Updates** | Price changes require developer intervention |
| **Inconsistent Information** | Product details vary across channels |

## 4. Scope Definition

### 4.1 In Scope

| Capability | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **View Products** | List all available hosting packages |
| **View Details** | See individual product features and pricing |
| **Create Product** | Admin creates new products |
| **Update Product** | Admin modifies product details |
| **Remove Product** | Admin deactivates products |

### 4.2 Out of Scope

| Capability | Handled By |
|------------|------------|
| Cart operations | Cart Management (2.1.7) |
| Purchasing | Order Management (2.1.8) |
| Product images | Future phase |

## 6. Business Rules

| Rule ID | Rule | Rationale |
|---------|------|-----------|
| BR-PRD-001 | All prices displayed in ZAR | South African market |
| BR-PRD-002 | Products must have at least one feature | Customer clarity |
| BR-PRD-003 | Price must be greater than zero | Valid product |
| BR-PRD-004 | Only active products visible to customers | Hide discontinued items |
