## 1. Executive Summary

### 1.1 Purpose

The **Contact Management** capability enables website visitors to submit inquiries through a contact form. Messages are routed to the appropriate team (General, Sales, Support) and senders receive confirmation of receipt.

### 1.2 Key Benefits

| Benefit | Business Impact |
|---------|-----------------|
| **24/7 Availability** | Customers contact anytime |
| **Automatic Routing** | Messages reach correct team |
| **Instant Confirmation** | Customers know message received |
| **Complete Records** | All inquiries stored for reference |

---

## 2. Business Problem

| Problem | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| **Email-Only Contact** | Requires customer to open email client |
| **No Routing** | All inquiries go to single inbox |
| **Lost Messages** | No confirmation of receipt |

---

## 3. Business Value

| Metric | Before | After | Improvement |
|--------|--------|-------|-------------|
| **Response Time** | Variable | Tracked | Measurable |
| **Customer Reach** | Email users | All visitors | Expanded |
| **Message Tracking** | None | Complete | Full audit |

## 4. Scope Definition

### 4.1 In Scope

| Capability | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **Submit Form** | Visitor submits contact inquiry |
| **Select Type** | Choose General, Sales, or Support |
| **Confirmation** | Receive acknowledgment email |
| **Team Notification** | Appropriate team alerted |

### 4.2 Out of Scope

| Capability | Handled By |
|------------|------------|
| Ticket management | External CRM |
| Response handling | Manual process |
| File attachments | Future phase |
